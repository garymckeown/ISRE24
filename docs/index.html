About the Conference

We are happy to announce that the International Society for Research on Emotion (ISRE) conference will take place in-person on July 17-20, 2024 at Queenâ€™s University Belfast, Northern Ireland.

The ISRE conference is an exciting opportunity to meet international colleagues, present your work, and stay up-to-date with the latest developments in emotion research.
